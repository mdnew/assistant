# BD Process Playbook

*Claude will follow this process when helping with BD activities.*

## Outreach

1. Research the company and key contact (use `stakeholders/roster.md` for existing context)
2. Identify the right angle — what's their pain, what's our hook?
3. Draft a short, personalized first message (email or LinkedIn)
4. Log the contact in `stakeholders/roster.md`
5. Add to pipeline in `deals/pipeline.md` at "Prospecting"

## Discovery Call

- Run `/prep [name]` before every call
- Goals: understand their situation, qualify fit, surface urgency
- After: run `/meetsync` or paste transcript to capture notes and actions

## Proposal

- Draft in `drafts/` before sending
- Get review before sending anything important
- Log in pipeline when sent → move to "Proposal" stage

## Negotiation

- Log all decisions with `/decide`
- Track every commitment made (by both sides) as action items

## Close

- Update pipeline to "Closed Won" or "Closed Lost"
- If lost, document the reason — this is valuable data

## Follow-up Cadence

- After first outreach: follow up in 3-5 business days if no response
- After a call: follow up same day or next morning
- Active deals: at minimum weekly touch
