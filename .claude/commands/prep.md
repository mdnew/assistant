# Meeting / Call Preparation

Prepare me for a meeting or call with: $ARGUMENTS

## Instructions

1. **Look up the person** in `stakeholders/roster.md`
   - Their role, company, relationship history, what they care about
   - Any notes from previous interactions

2. **Find previous meeting notes** in `meetings/notes/` for any past interactions
   - Highlight follow-ups, commitments, or unresolved items

3. **Check deal status** in **HubSpot** (company or contact) if this is a BD/sales context; read **`deals/pipeline.md`** for **notes and context** on that deal

4. **Check pending action items** in `meetings/actions/` related to them

5. **Check calendar** using `GOOGLECALENDAR_EVENTS_LIST` to confirm meeting time and any other context in the invite

6. **Format the output**:

```
# Prep: [Name] — [Date/Time]

## Who They Are
[Role, company, how we know them, what they care about]

## Last Interaction
[Key points from most recent meeting/email]

## Pending Items
- [ ] Any open commitments from previous interactions

## Deal Context (if applicable)
[Stage, key issues, our position]

## Suggested Agenda / Talking Points
1. [Most important topic]
2. [Second topic]
3. [Nice to cover if time]

## What I Want to Walk Away With
[The one thing I need from this meeting]

## Things to Remember
[Anything specific about this person or situation]
```
