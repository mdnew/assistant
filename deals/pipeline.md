## Stages
- **Prospecting** — Identified, not yet contacted
- **Qualifying** — Initial conversations, assessing fit
- **Proposal** — Proposal sent or in progress
- **Appointment Scheduled** — Call or meeting booked
- **Presentation Scheduled** — Proposal/presentation confirmed
- **Contract Sent** — Terms out, awaiting signature
- **Closed Won** — Deal signed
- **Closed Lost** — Deal lost (note reason)
- **On Hold** — Paused, revisit date noted
